About Me

Poyraz's Website for Exploring Istanbul from an Art Perspective

If you also like visiting different cities as locals, here is the place! This website will play a tour guide role for expressing the art museums and modern exhibitions.

Obviously, Istanbul's touristic and historical destinations are very popular. Addition to the well-known touristic spots, I would like to focus on art destinations, which will improve your connection with the locals and global art followers, as well. You can discover hidden corners, urban gatherings and memorable adventure.

Also, if you want to explore independent art studios of the city with talented artists, this website could help you to reach them by following art events from different art perspectives. You will be amazed by the modern art spirit of the city.
